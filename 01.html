Working with Image

On the Insert tab, the galleries include items that are designed to coordinate with the overall look of your document. You can use these galleries to insert tables, headers, footers, lists, cover pages, and other document building blocks. When you create pictures, charts or diagrams they also coordinate with your current document look. You can easily change the formatting of selected text in your document by choosing a look for selected text from Quick Styles gallery on Home tab. You can also format directly by using other controls on Home tab.

Most controls offer choice of using looks from current theme or using format that you specify directly. To change the overall look of your documents choose new Theme elements on Page Layout tab. To change looks available in Quick Style gallery use Change Current Quick Style Set command. Both Themes gallery & Quick Styles gallery provide reset commands so that you always restore looks of your documents to original contained in current templates.
